PROGRAM OVERVIEW

FARMING FOR LIFE

Sign Up / Enquire

Farming for Life, a 16-lesson Book of Hope and Leader’s Guide combination, is an adapted version of the Foundations for Farming program for use within the classroom rather than the garden. This program works to teach students Biblical truths while also learning important farming techniques needed to sustainably grow food for their families and communities.

Program Outcomes:

  1. Understanding from Scripture that God sent his Son, Jesus, to save people from their sins, and that he works through people to change the earth for good.
  2. Understanding of conservation methods of farming.
  3. Their desire to apply God’s gifts in their own lives and to use them to improve the agricultural and spiritual life in their communities.

A BETTER WAY TO LIFE

Sign Up / Enquire

This book is designed to help teens dig deeper into God's Word and into topics that are important in their daily lives. Some revelant topics discussed in this course are: social media addiction, sexulaity, and mental health. These may be sensitive in nature to many teens which means creating a safe space within the classrooms will be very important. These lessons are intended to be used in a church or in a school programme to help 13-13 year olds for a series of one-hour classes, meeting weekly for nine weeks.

Program Outcomes:

  1. Teens will recognize that they are made by God in his image, and their identity, worth, and purpose are rooted in this truth.
  2. Teens will identify God as the ultimate source of all the truth and recognize the Bible as God's trustworthy Word which is full of guidance for our lives.
  3. Teens will recall how God's Word reveals the truth about key issues in their lives (sexuality/gender, mental health, social media addiction, identity etc.)

A WORLD WITHOUT SICKNESS

Sign Up / Enquire

A workshop for awareness about menstruation and menstrual hygiene, so that girls know what to do when it hits them. Knowing what will happen during puberty is the first step towards adapting well to adolescence.
This programme has six lessons of approximately 1 hour each, and covers important to be such a such as safe drinking water, clean food preparation, staying worm-free, using a laterine, and oral hygiene. Also included in this program is biblical teaching on how to keep your heart right before a loving and forgiving God.

Program Outcomes:

  1. Grow in their understanding that God loves and values them and has demonstrated His great love in sending Jesus to cleanse them from their sins.
  2. Recall healthy habits that can be incorporated into their daily lives, to help prevent significant diseases or health concerns.
  3. Practise good health and hygiene habits in their daily lives and share what they have learnt with others to affect the overall health of their famalies and communities.

CALLED TO THRIVE

Sign Up / Enquire

This programme helps young people learn about their unique strengths, passions, and resources. They will work through what Scripture teaches about God's call on their life and his vision for how they are prepared for good works. With scriptural wisdom and discovery tools, young people will be encouraged to think deeply abouth the next stage of their life. As they discover more about themselves, they will also be empowered to live out their call from God. The recommended way of executing this program, is a one hour lesson each week for 16 weeks to the intended group of 16-21 year-olds

Program Outcomes:

  1. Demonstrate an understanding of God's call to work and how it impacts their present thinking, confidence, and character.
  2. Engage in activities that include practical training and skills.
  3. Understand God's call to work is a call to thrive as they follow him.

EMPOWERED

Sign Up / Enquire

The Empowered Worldview program was designed to empower children to live out their God-given purpose and make a difference in the world. God has created each child in His image and has a good plan for their lives. As you walk through this program with children you will help them to see the world and their place in it through the lens of Scripture and God’s plan. The lesson are designed for a series of up to one-hour classes per week for eight weeks. The material can also be presented in a variety of ways to meet your needs, taken into account the background of your participants, the setting of sessions, and the time available.

Program Outcomes:

  1. Understand that although the world may be broken, the Lord has empowered them with a purpose to make a difference in it.
  2. Learn how to use Scripture, prayer, and community to discover and live out their corporate and individual purpose.

LIGHT IN THE DARK

Sign Up / Enquire

This program was designed to tell the story of a little girl called Zuri. Zuri was like many children across Africa who live in fear, however as she grew in her understanding of who God is, she was able to make baby steps to let go of fear and embrace the love God had for her. Zuri’s journey teaches children that perfect love drives out all fear. The program is intended to show the little children that there is power in being little, that their life is not meant to be covered in fear and they can speak to Jesus at any time, anywhere.

Program Outcomes:

  1. Describe how Jesus’ love builds their confidence and shows them God’s victory
  2. Recognize that it is okay to feel afraid, but God does not want us to stay in that fear
