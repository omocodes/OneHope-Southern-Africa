TRAINING PROGRAMS OVERVIEW

17 STORIES

Sign Up / Enquire

17 Stories Is a unique tool created by OneHope that uses individual Bible Stories and the metanarrative of the Bible to reinforce the way each story in the Bible fits into the larger framework of God’s “Big Story”. When we refer to God’s Big Story, we are referring to the Metanarrative of scripture that means the Biblical story from creation to the death and resurrection of Jesus. So, once the children have completed the program, they would be familiar with 16 stories from the Bible in chronological order from the story of creation to the death and resurrection of Jesus.

Program Outcomes:

  1. Share the Gospel.
  2. Promote consistent attendance by children in a discipleship group.
  3. Explain the overarching theme of the Bible through chronological Bible storytelling.
  4. Teach and encourage children to retell Bible stories.

STORIES OF HOPE

Sign Up / Enquire

Stories of Hope was designed to be used with children ages 6-12 years old and can easily be implemented in your Sunday School or Bible Club. The material will challenge children with God’s Word and touch some of the deepest needs of their hearts, including their most basic need for salvation. It will help them seek, know, and understand the nature and character of the one true God.

Designed for three main purposes:

  1. Children will learn about and be able to communicate God’s Big Story.
  2. Children will be able to address key issues from a Biblical perspective and provide guidance on how to live by the truths found in God`s word
  3. To create an opportunity for parents and children to interact while learning Biblical content.

Created in partnership with EQUIP, John Maxwell’s leadership foundation. Lead Today is a 15-week leadership course, made specifically for teenagers ages 13- 18 years old and focuses on 14 leadership qualities taught through the lens of Biblical examples and Maxwell’s leadership wisdom.

JOURNEY TO HOPE

Sign Up / Enquire

TIn Partnership with World Vision in their campaign to address violence against children and out of this partnership we designed and developed this program. Through Journey to Hope you can initiate conversation, influence communities, and see the hearts of the abused, the abuser and the friend transformed by the love of Jesus! Violence against children is happening everywhere. In all of our communities it may look different and it may be hiding in deep darkness. The Journey to Hope program has been designed to shine light into these dark places and restore hope in the next generation

Program Outcomes:

  1. Jesus loves them, He knows their story, and He can bring comfort, forgiveness, and an end to shame as their fellowship with God is restored.
  2. By sharing their story with trustworthy friends and leaders, they can experience healing and be empowered to speak truth and respond in love to the needs of others.
  3. They are loved and valued by God, with inherent worth above all else, so they should honor God with their bodies and protect themselves and others.

FOUNDATIONS FOR FARMING

Sign Up / Enquire

Foundations for Farming started many years ago when the farm which Brian Oldrieve was managing was nearing bankruptcy. Common farming practices on the farm involved burning along with deep soil inversion which resulted in terrible sheet erosion which resulted in the loss of seed and water. They found themselves spending more and more money on machinery required for ploughing and double rolling of lands, but their yields were declining. Being a man of faith Brian cried out to God to help him in his dire situation...

Program Outcomes:

  1. Learn how God is faithfully at work in their lives and their world, even during times of hardship.
  2. Understand the key points of the Gospel message.
  3. Learn Biblical values that guide them in gardening and farming.
  4. Gain experience, practical skills, and confidence in gardening and farming.
  5. Use the farming principles and methods taught in the program in gardens or farms at home, at church,or in their community.
